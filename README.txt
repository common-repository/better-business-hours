== Description ==

Easily set and display your business hours. A shortcode and widget are included so you can put it anywhere on your site.

== Installation ==
Install & Activate plugin, then go to "Business Hours" in the main wp-admin menu. Your hours default to 9-5 Monday through Friday, simply click/drag different timeslots to set & unset your availability.

To display your business hours on your site, simply use the shortcode [business_hours] anywhere on your site, or use the Business Hours widget to place it in a sidebar.

= Manual Installation =

1. Upload the entire `/better-business-hours` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate Better Business Hours through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I change my hours? =
Simply click on "Business Hours" in the wp-admin menu, and click/drag on the grid to set your avaialibility. Changes are immediately saved and displayed on the frontend of the site wherever you have the widget or shortcode placed.

= Can I specify a lunch break or unusual hours? =
Absolutely! Just erase the hours you'll be unavailable

= How do I display my hours on the frontend? =
Use our shortcode [business_hours] anywhere in your content, or use the included Business Hours widget
